# Quick weeknight pasta — 20 minutes

A demo recipe with knowable specifics for the mnemos walkthrough.

## Ingredients

- **400 g** dried pasta (spaghetti or linguine)
- **6 cloves** garlic, sliced thin
- **2 tablespoons** olive oil
- **1 teaspoon** crushed red pepper flakes
- Generous fresh parsley
- Parmesan, to taste
- Salt

## Method

1. Boil pasta in salted water for 9 minutes; reserve 1 cup pasta water.
2. While pasta cooks, warm olive oil over low heat with garlic and red pepper flakes for 5 minutes (don't brown the garlic).
3. Drain pasta, toss with garlic oil, add pasta water as needed.
4. Top with parsley and parmesan. Serve immediately.

## Notes

Serves 4. Total time: 20 minutes. Cost per serving: about $1.80.
