AURORA ONE — CUSTOMER SUPPORT FAQ
Last updated: 2026-04-18

------------------------------------------------------------
WARRANTY
------------------------------------------------------------
The Aurora One carries a 2-year limited warranty from the date of purchase.
The warranty covers manufacturing defects in the brew head, pump, and solar
panel. It does NOT cover consumable parts (the piston seal and the portafilter
gasket) or damage from descaling neglect.

The piston seal is a consumable. Under normal use (one to three brews per day)
it should be replaced every 12 months. Replacement seals ship free of charge
for the first two years; after that they are $9 per pack of three.

------------------------------------------------------------
RETURNS
------------------------------------------------------------
Unopened units may be returned within 30 days for a full refund. Opened units
that have been used may be returned within 30 days for store credit, provided
the solar panel and brew head are undamaged. Return shipping is free within the
continental US.

------------------------------------------------------------
TROUBLESHOOTING
------------------------------------------------------------
Q: The water won't reach brew temperature.
A: Most heat-up complaints trace to panel angle. Aim the panel directly at the
   sun — a 30-degree misalignment roughly doubles heat-up time. In full sun the
   chamber reaches 92 C in about 4 minutes; in overcast light expect ~9 minutes.

Q: The shot tastes weak or runs too fast.
A: Grind finer. The Aurora One targets 9 bar; too coarse a grind drops pressure
   and the shot runs in under 20 seconds instead of the intended 28.

Q: Coffee leaks around the portafilter.
A: The portafilter gasket has likely hardened. Replace it. Gaskets are a
   consumable and are not covered under warranty.

------------------------------------------------------------
DESCALING (do this monthly)
------------------------------------------------------------
1. Dissolve one teaspoon of citric acid in the full 120 ml chamber.
2. Run a blank brew cycle (no coffee) with the blind disc installed.
3. Rinse twice with clean water.
Skipping descaling is the single most common cause of pump failure and voids
the warranty on the pump.
